Metric Definitions

Full SQL, source tables, and formulas: metric_definitions.md in this folder. Query: country_scorecard.sql · Project: data-eng-prod-431217

Active Users

Rolling 30 days

COUNT(DISTINCT user_id) where is_completed = TRUE. Source: int_remittances.int__remittances, filtered on payment_created_at. Country: destination_country_code.

New User Conversion Rate

Rolling 30 days

user_id_converted ÷ user_id × 100. Source: mart_chat__funnel_conversion_user_level. Filters: funnel = 'full_funnel', user_type = 'New User', date window. Omni-aligned.

Existing User Conversion Rate

Rolling 30 days

Same as new user CVR with user_type = 'Existing User'. Same mart, date, and country logic. Omni-aligned.

Disbursement Success Rate

Rolling 14 days

Successful Disbursement attempts ÷ all attempts × 100. Source: int__disbursements joined to int__remittances. Filtered on created_at_est / payment_created_at.

Bot Failures

Rolling 7 days

sessions_with_bot_failure ÷ total_sessions × 100. Source: mart__automation_rate, created_at. Higher is worse. Green <3%, yellow 3–10%, red >10%.

NPS (Net Promoter Score)

Rolling 30 days

% Promoters − % Detractors from int__nps_survey_responses. Neutrals excluded from score. Small samples in PE, DO, EC.

Chargeback Rate

Rolling 14 days

Disputed payments ÷ completed payments × 100. Source: int__remittances. Filter: is_last_disbursement_for_payment_id = TRUE. Payments and disputes both in 14d window.
