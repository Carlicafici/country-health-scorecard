Metric Definitions

Full SQL, source tables, and formulas: metric_definitions.md in this folder. Query: country_scorecard.sql · Project: data-eng-prod-431217

Active Users

Rolling 30 days

Distinct users who completed at least one transaction in the last 30 days. COUNT(DISTINCT user_id) where is_completed = TRUE. Source: int_remittances.int__remittances, filtered on payment_created_at. Country: destination_country_code.

New User Conversion Rate

Rolling 7 days

Of the new users who entered the full chat funnel in the last 7 days, the % who completed a transaction. COUNT(DISTINCT user_id_converted) ÷ COUNT(DISTINCT user_id) × 100. Source: mart_chat__funnel_conversion_user_level. Filters: funnel = 'full_funnel', user_type = 'New User'. Country: default_remittance_country. Omni-aligned.

Existing User Conversion Rate

Rolling 7 days

Of the existing users (≥ 1 prior transaction) who entered the full chat funnel in the last 7 days, the % who completed a transaction. Same formula, mart, and country logic as new user CVR with user_type = 'Existing User'. Omni-aligned.

Disbursement Success Rate

Rolling 14 days

Of all disbursement attempts in the last 14 days, the % that resulted in a successful payout. Successful Disbursement attempts ÷ all attempts × 100. Source: int__disbursements joined to int__remittances. Filtered on created_at_est / payment_created_at.

Bot Failures

Rolling 7 days

Of all chat sessions in the last 7 days, the % that required human operator escalation. sessions_with_bot_failure ÷ total_sessions × 100. Source: mart__automation_rate, created_at. Higher is worse. Green <3%, yellow 3–10%, red >10%.

NPS (Net Promoter Score)

Rolling 30 days

Survey sentiment over the last 30 days: % Promoters − % Detractors from int__nps_survey_responses. Neutrals stay in the denominator but not the score. Small samples in PE, DO, EC.

Chargeback Rate

Rolling 14 days

Of completed payments in the last 14 days, the % that had a chargeback or dispute filed in the same window. Disputed payments ÷ completed payments × 100. Source: int__remittances. Filter: is_last_disbursement_for_payment_id = TRUE. Payments and disputes both in 14d window.
